Educational Terms Dictionary (Arabic - English)

Develop Educational Terms Dictionary Enable users (students - teachers - researcher) to search about any educational terms in arabic and english languages, And share their experiences in building a free and easy dictionary that allows them to add and modify and develop cumulatively.

Project Features

Easy search allows students, teachers and researchers to search and define educational terms and translate them into arabic correctly.
Allow experts and specialists to add educational terms.
Allow supervisor and administrators in ABEGS To reviews and approves educational terms data.
Allow end users to search the dictionary and add notes as well as save educational term in his own favourites.
